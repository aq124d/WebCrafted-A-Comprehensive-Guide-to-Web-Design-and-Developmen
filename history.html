    1989

  • Tim Berners-Lee builds the WordlWideWeb, the first browser,using ideas he devised in 1989. In 1990, he opens the browser.[12]

    • 1990

  • Alan Emtage, Peter Deutsch, and Bill Heelen were the three students that create the first search engine. It was a huge turning point in the history of Internet.[12]
  • In the same year, graphic editor Adobe Photoshop appeared on the market.However,in the beginning Photoshop was only available for Macintosh computers and required a RAM of 2 MB.[12]

    • 1991

  • In 1991, Berners-Lee launched the first web-page using HTML commands.The web-page had a basic formatting such as white page and black text. Nevertheless, the fundamental codes that he used are also used by modern day designers.[12]
  • HTTP protocol which is used for exchanging hypertext documents between the server and the computer is also the created by Berners-Lee.[12]

    • 1992

  • Pei-Yuan Wei's ViolaWWW browser,created while he was employed at the University of California, is a noteworthy development of this year. This browser had a stylesheet to specify how the website should look and supported forms, scripting, and table rendering.[12]
  • BBEdit HTML and text editor was released by Bare Bones Software for Macintosh to help the web developers.[12]
  • Scientist Silvano De Gennar of CERN posts the first image online, which is a picture of the spoof band Les Horribles Cernettes.[12]
  • There was an additional search engine launched this year.The name of the search engine is Veronica which is short for 'Very Easy Rodent-Oriented Net-wide Index to Computer Archives.'[12]

    • 1993

  • The first image-based web browser,Mosaic,and the 1993 search engine,ALIWEB,were released this year.Bright,colourful imagery adorned the originak landing page, which was designed by MTV's Video Journalist Adam Curry and was a major turning point in web design.[12]

    • 1994

  • Significant changes occurred on the web in 1994,including the introduction of the first banner ad on the Hotwire website, the appearance of an HTML validator, the founding of th World Wide Web Consortium by Tim Berners-Lee, and the development of the Comic Sans font, which is well-known for its readable style and teaching strategies that cater to dyslexia.[12]

    • 1995

  • Javascript was created in 1995 by Brendan Eich to support dynamic user interfaces in Netscape 2. With the release of HTML 2.0, programmers could now construct images forms, and tables. The phrase "user experience" was first used during a conference in Denver,Colarado, which signalled a major breakthrough in technology.[12]

  • HÃ¥kon Wium introduced CSS, making the table-based design more user-friendly. Microsoft Internet Explorer 3 supports and makes CSS the mainstream. Late 1990s internet was impacted because of Flash 10. introducing vector animations.[12]

    • 1997-1998

  • Significant technological advancements during this time were Dreamweaver, GoLive, Macromedia Fireworks, and Google, which had a big impact on web design by making it more approachable for non-technical people and solidifying Google's position as the industry standard for user-relevant search results worldwide.[12]

    • 2000-2004

  • A total of 36 million people used websites at this time, including well-known ones like Deviant Art, Google, Yahoo!, WordPress, Wikipedia, and PayPal. Content management systems gained popularity and web design got more robust. Yourspace and Myspace, the first social networking platforms, introduced maximalist design principles. When Web 2.0 was initially coined in 1998, social medial, forums, and picture sharing were prioritised.[12]

    • 2004-2007

  • Without knowing HTML,users constructed websites that eventually led to the creation of the modern internet, which includes well-known sites like Facebook, YouTube, Reddit, and Flickr. During this period, the internet landscape experienced a transformative shift as individuals, often without extensive technical knowledge, actively contributed to the growth of online communities, paving the way for the dynamic and interconnected digital era we experience today.[12]

    • 2007-2013

  • The popularity of the iPhone forced a transition away from Flash compatibility with Apple and towards cross-platform support and mobile application creation. 2010 saw the rise of React, open-source toolkit that made designing user interfaces for web apps easier, and the necessity of responsive design for web designers.[12]

    • 2013-2021

  • Although developers have specialised in JavaScript,HTML, and CSS for distinct purposes,HTML and CSS are still widely used in website design.HTML is necessary for cross-platform development, but CSS is necessary for interesting content.In 2017, HTML 5.0 offered greater versatility, rendering Flash obsolete. In 2019, more people use mobile devices to access the internet than desktops.[12]

    • Web Design in 2022

  • In 2022, classic typefaces and simple designs are back in style, and JavaScript is utilised in many well-known apps and websites. Multi-platform websites with expansive landing pages, art deco, retro-futuristic, and abstract images are the goal.[12]
